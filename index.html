Astronomy & Astrophysics Ph.D. candidate at Michigan State University interested in computational astrophysics.

My advisor is Prof. Sean Couch and my current/past collaborators include: Prof. Frank Timmes (ASU), Dr. Robert Farmer (ASU), Dr. Ilka Petermann (ASU), and Prof. Christian Iliadis (UNC).

My research interests focus on different aspects of computational nuclear astrophysics. This includes compact objects, astrophysical sources of gravitational waves, and core collape supernovae with emphasis on progenitor evolution, explosion, and nucleosynthesis.
