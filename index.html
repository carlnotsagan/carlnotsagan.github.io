Dr. Carl E. Fields

I am an RPF Distinguished Postdoctoral Fellow at Los Alamos National Laboratory in the Computational Physics and Methods (CCS-2) and Eulerian Codes (XCP-2) Divisions.
My research interests include stellar evolution and hydrodynamics, core-collapse supernova explosions with emphasis on their progenitors, and astrophysical sources of gravitational waves.
